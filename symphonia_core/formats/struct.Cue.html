A Cue is a designated point of time within a media stream.

A Cue may be a mapping from either a source track, a chapter, cuesheet, or a timestamp depending on the source media. A Cueโs duration is the difference between the Cueโs timestamp and the next. Each Cue may contain an optional index of points relative to the Cue that never exceed the timestamp of the next Cue. A Cue may also have associated Tags.
