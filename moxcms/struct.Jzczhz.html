Expand description

Represents Jzazbz in polar coordinates as Jzczhz

Fields§

§jz: f32

Jz(lightness) generally expects to be between 0.0..1.0.

§cz: f32

Cz generally expects to be between -1.0..1.0.

§hz: f32

Hz generally expects to be between -1.0..1.0.
